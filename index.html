About

The African Leadership University provides higher education for a higher purpose. ALU was founded to put excellent education within reach of all passionate and driven students. Our unique approach equips our graduates with the skills, acuity and vision needed to succeed as ethical, entrepreneurial leaders, and our vibrant, Pan-African community promises lifelong friendship and inspiration.
